Common Challenges in Traditional International Payments

For too long, businesses and individuals have faced significant challenges with international payments that disrupt cash flow, create uncertainty, and add unnecessary costs:

  • Slow Processing Times: Traditional bank transfers can take 2-5 business days to clear, causing significant delays in receiving funds.
  • High and Hidden Fees: Multiple intermediary banks take a cut, and opaque fee structures often include hidden charges and poor exchange rates.
  • Lack of Transparency: It's often difficult to track the status of a payment in real-time, leaving both sender and receiver in the dark.
  • Complex and Manual Processes: Navigating the complex requirements of different banking systems can be a frustrating and time-consuming experience.
