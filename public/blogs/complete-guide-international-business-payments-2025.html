Cost Optimization Strategies

Hidden Costs to Watch

  • • Exchange rate markups (1-4%)
  • • Intermediary bank fees ($10-25)
  • • Correspondent bank charges
  • • Receiving bank fees ($15-30)
  • • Currency conversion fees

Cost Reduction Strategies

  • • Use digital payment platforms
  • • Negotiate better rates for volume
  • • Time payments for favorable rates
  • • Consolidate smaller payments
  • • Choose direct routing options

Best Practices for Success

1

Audit Current Payment Processes

Identify inefficiencies and hidden costs in your existing setup. Document all fees, processing times, and failure rates.

2

Evaluate Modern Alternatives

Test digital platforms and compare total costs, not just fees. Consider speed, reliability, and customer support quality.

3

Implement Gradually

Start with smaller payments to test new systems before full migration. This reduces risk and allows for process refinement.

4

Monitor and Optimize

Continuously track performance and adjust strategies based on results. Set up dashboards to monitor costs and processing times.
